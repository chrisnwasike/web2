# 30-Day Client Acquisition Plan
### Conversion Setup Freelance — Full Execution Guide

> **One offer. One funnel. One repeatable system.**

## THE OFFER (memorize this)

> **"3-day mobile conversion setup for Instagram and Facebook businesses."**

Never say "web design." Say **conversion setup**.

**What's included:**
- [ ] One landing page or mini-site
- [ ] WhatsApp + call CTA
- [ ] Product/service showcase
- [ ] Testimonials and trust section
- [ ] Mobile speed cleanup
- [ ] Basic analytics
- [ ] Optional Facebook/Instagram lead flow

**Pricing (non-negotiable floors):**
| Tier | Price | Use when |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | ₦150,000 | Simple one-page, minimal content |
| Standard | ₦250,000 | Full mini-site, WhatsApp flow |
| Premium | ₦350,000 | Full build + lead flow + analytics |

> **Rule:** Never go below ₦150k. Below that = disrespect + scope creep.

---

## THE LEAD SHEET (set this up before Day 1)

Create a spreadsheet with these exact columns:

| Business Name | Platform | Contact | City | Niche | Problem Spotted | Last Post Date | Followers | Score (1–5) | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

**Score guide:**
- 5 = Active posts + obvious weak buying path + visible demand
- 4 = Active posts + no website or broken site
- 3 = Moderate activity, one clear problem
- 2 = Slow posting, unclear niche
- 1 = Dead or polished — skip

**Target 50 leads before Day 1 outreach begins.**

---

## LEAD FILTERS (only pursue if 4+ match)

- [ ] Posting actively (within 7 days)
- [ ] Visible demand (comments, DMs, "how much?")
- [ ] Weak or no website
- [ ] Bad mobile experience
- [ ] Clear product or service
- [ ] WhatsApp as only sales channel
- [ ] Poor trust signals (no reviews, no testimonials)
- [ ] Real followers (not obviously fake)

**Skip immediately if:**
- Dead account (last post 30+ days ago)
- No visible product or service
- Already polished and clearly doing well
- Only posting "collab" requests
- Asking for cheapest possible price before you've said anything

## THE OUTREACH SEQUENCE (4 messages, spread over 7 days)

> Follow this sequence exactly. Do not compress it. Do not skip to price.

```
Message 1 (Day 3)   → Observation only
Message 2 (Day 5)   → Problem framing
Message 3 (Day 7)   → Offer the fix
Message 4 (Day 9)   → Price + timeline
```

**The framework:** Attention → Relevance → Trust → Offer

---

## FULL MESSAGE SCRIPTS

### Message 1 — Soft Observation

**Variant A (general):**
```
Your page has demand, but the buying path is a bit weak. I noticed one fix that would make ordering easier for people coming from your posts.
```

**Variant B (specific to content):**
```
Your product looks good. The page is probably losing people between interest and checkout — it happens a lot with Instagram traffic.
```

**Variant C (Google Maps / no website):**
```
Saw your listing on Maps. Strong reviews, but the contact info looks outdated and there's no easy way for new customers to order. That's likely costing you walk-ins.
```

> **Rule:** One sentence observation. One sentence implication. Stop there.

---

### Message 2 — Problem Framing (send only if they reply or engage)

```
I can show you a faster buying flow that works better from Instagram and Facebook traffic. Most of the drop-off happens because people have to work too hard to order.
```

---

### Message 3 — Offer the Fix

```
I do this in 3 days. If you want, I can send a quick breakdown of what's leaking and what I'd fix first.
```

---

### Message 4 — Price and Timeline (after they express interest)

```
Based on what I saw, the fastest fix is a mobile conversion setup. Depending on what you need, it starts from ₦150k.

Delivery is 3 days after content and deposit are in. Want me to put together a one-page breakdown?
```

---

### The Mini Audit Message (for hot leads only)

Send this after Message 1 gets a positive reply:

```
Quick audit on what I noticed:

PROBLEM 1: [specific issue — e.g. "No clear link in bio, forcing buyers to DM just to find the price"]
PROBLEM 2: [specific issue — e.g. "No testimonials visible — trust is low for first-time buyers"]
PROBLEM 3: [specific issue — e.g. "Mobile load is slow, people are dropping off before they see your products"]

FIX 1: [brief fix — e.g. "Direct landing page with product showcase and WhatsApp CTA"]
FIX 2: [brief fix — e.g. "Trust section with 3–5 testimonials above the fold"]
FIX 3: [brief fix — e.g. "Lightweight mobile build with fast image loading"]

RESULT: Buyers go from seeing your post → landing page → WhatsApp order in under 60 seconds.

Want me to put this into a one-page proposal?
```

## THE 30-DAY DAILY PLAN

### PRE-WORK (Before Day 1)
- [ ] Create lead sheet with all 10 columns
- [ ] Write your offer one-liner (memorize it)
- [ ] Lock in your three pricing tiers
- [ ] Save all message scripts somewhere you can paste from fast

---

### DAY 1 — Build the List
- [ ] Find 50 leads across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Google Maps, LinkedIn
- [ ] Score each lead 1–5
- [ ] Write one sentence per lead: what is broken
- [ ] Finalize your offer and pricing tiers

---

### DAY 2 — Warm Up Top 20 Leads
- [ ] Follow the 20 highest-scored leads
- [ ] Like 2–3 recent posts per account
- [ ] Watch a story if one is active
- [ ] React once if it feels natural — no pitching yet

> **Goal:** Become a familiar face, not a cold stranger.

---

### DAY 3 — First Outreach Wave
- [ ] Send Message 1 to 15 leads (Observation only)
- [ ] Log each contact in your sheet
- [ ] Update status column for everyone messaged

---

### DAY 4 — Qualify Replies
- [ ] Check who replied
- [ ] Ask one qualifier per hot lead:
  - "Are you actively trying to improve sales right now?"
  - "Do you have a launch date you're working toward?"
  - "Do you already have product photos and content ready?"
- [ ] For hot leads: send the mini audit (3 problems / 3 fixes / 1 outcome)

---

### DAY 5 — Push Toward a Call or Direct Close
- [ ] Send Message 2 to engaged leads
- [ ] Offer the cheapest viable starting point only if they push on price
- [ ] Log all replies and update statuses

---

### DAY 6 — Send Proposals
- [ ] Send proposals only to warm leads (replied at least twice)
- [ ] Use the one-page proposal template
- [ ] Each proposal = specific to their actual problem, not generic

---

### DAY 7 — Close Attempt
- [ ] Send close message to all leads who have a proposal
- [ ] One deliverable. One timeline. One price. One deposit.
- [ ] Mark lead status as: Closed / Pending / Dead

---

### DAYS 8–10 — Scale the Process
Daily targets:
- [ ] 20 new leads added to sheet
- [ ] 5 follow-ups sent
- [ ] 3 warm comments or reactions
- [ ] 2 fresh audits drafted
- [ ] 1 proposal sent

---

### DAYS 11–14 — Sharpen the Niche
- [ ] Review which niche replied fastest
- [ ] Review which message got the most responses
- [ ] Cut the two weakest niches from your search
- [ ] Double outreach volume in your top-performing niche

**Most likely winner will be one of:**
- Wine and spirits
- Fashion
- Skincare
- Salons
- Restaurants
- Gadget stores

---

### DAYS 15–21 — Deliver and Capture Proof
For every active client:
- [ ] Do not overbuild — deliver exactly what was scoped
- [ ] Finish on or before the deadline (72hr rule)
- [ ] Mobile experience must be excellent
- [ ] Ordering path must be under 3 taps
- [ ] Request written testimonial immediately on launch
- [ ] Request permission to screenshot before/after
- [ ] Ask for one referral

---

### DAYS 22–30 — Build Recurring Revenue
- [ ] Offer retainer to every client delivered
- [ ] Use the retainer message script above
- [ ] Begin a second round of outreach with new leads
- [ ] Build your proof library (screenshots, testimonials, metrics)
- [ ] Raise pricing for new clients if you have 2+ testimonials

---

## YOUR DAILY ROUTINE (non-negotiable)

| Block | Time | Task |
|---|---|---|
| Lead search | 60 min | Find and score new leads |
| Outreach | 60 min | Send messages, follow-ups |
| Follow-up | 30 min | Reply to active conversations |
| Audit notes | 30 min | Write problem/fix notes for warm leads |
| Proof building | 30 min | Delivery, screenshots, testimonials |

**Total: 3.5 hours/day.** The rest of your day is yours.

---

## THE WIN CONDITION

You are not trying to get lucky.
You are building enough quality conversations that 1–2 close per week.

**The first money** comes from whichever niche responds best.
**The next move** is specializing in that niche and raising your price.
**The real goal** is a repeatable system that pays every month — not a one-off project.

---

## WHAT NOT TO DO (ever)

- ❌ Send a long introduction DM
- ❌ Talk about your experience or background unprompted
- ❌ Drop your portfolio before they show interest
- ❌ Beg for a chance
- ❌ Drop price before they push back
- ❌ Overload them with features
- ❌ Argue about affordability
- ❌ Chase a dead lead past Day 10
- ❌ Start work without a deposit
